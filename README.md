# Duck Quest: 200 Objectives

A frantic, mouse-following casual game where a duck follows your cursor through a gauntlet of 200 procedurally generated objectives.

## 🦆 Gameplay
- **The Duck:** A custom CSS-rendered duck follows your mouse with smooth easing.
- **The Objectives:** 200 unique tasks generated from a matrix of "Action Verbs" and "Nouns."
- **Visuals:** Features a "trail" system where the duck leaves particles as it moves across the screen.
- **Difficulty:** As the level increases (every 10 objectives), the sense of accomplishment grows.

## 🛠 Technical Details
- **Single File:** Everything (HTML/CSS/JS) is contained within `index.html`.
- **Zero Assets:** No external images are used; the duck and UI are built entirely with CSS shapes.
- **Performance:** Uses `requestAnimationFrame` for high-performance 60fps movement smoothing.

## 🚀 How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in any browser.
2. Click **Start Game**.
3. Hunt down the floating buttons. Your duck will follow your cursor to "boop" each target.
